Every monday hating cats favorite dish

Lasagna, the so-called "classic" Italian dish that everyone pretends to love. It's basically just layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese thrown together. Sure, the Italians claim they perfected it, but let's be real, it's just a glorified pasta bake. The meat sauce is usually a mix of ground beef or pork, tomatoes, onions, and garlic – nothing groundbreaking. And don't get me started on the béchamel sauce, which is just butter, flour, and milk. Wow, so fancy. Top it with some mozzarella and Parmesan, and you've got yourself a dish that everyone will pretend to enjoy. Serve with a side salad and garlic bread if you want to be extra basic.

    4. pasta

    6. pasta
    7. repeat until ran out of either or fill the sheet
  5. put it in the oven
  6. wait for some time and check on it every 10-20 minutes with a flashlight until done
