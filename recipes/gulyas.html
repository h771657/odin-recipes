Ingredients

  • one big oninon
  • beef shoulder
  • a big tomato
  • potatoes
  • paprika
  • garlic
  • carrots
  • red whine
  • spices
    • kumin
    • pepper
    • paprika
    • bay leaf
    • broth or stock cube or beef bouillon
  • optionals
    • whole chili
    • parsley root
    • "csipetke" pasta

Steps

  1. cut the beef into bite sized cubes
  2. start searing the beef on sime fat
    • fine dice the onion, the paprika and the tomato
  3. soup base
    • take out the seared meatcubes, deglaze the pot with the chopped veggies
    • add more fat if needed
    • when the veggies start to stick to the pot deglaze with wine
    • repeat 2-3 times
    • peel and chop the carrots and potatoes into bite sized bits
  4. add the ground paprika, pepper, garlic and bloom them for a minute
  5. add meat, bay leaf, parsley root, chilies and fill the pot with water
  6. add bay leaf and heavily salt the soup
  7. barely bubbling cook it for 1,5-2 hours
    • add more wine if needed
    • add more water if evaporated
    • csipetke
      • crack an egg into a small heap of flour
      • add some salt and work it into a dough
      • add more flour if too sticky
      • wrap the dough into plastic, and let the dough hidrate for 0,5-1 hour
  8. last touches
    • add the potatoes and carrots
    • add more salt if needed
    • add csipetke in the last 2-3 minutes
      • divide the dough into palm sized balls
      • flat out the dough and start pinching off bits into a bowl of flour
        • from time to time shake the bowl to cover every piece with flour so they won't stick to each other
